Our Story.

Applied by Design was founded on the idea that engineering web applications succeed when technical design requirements, user experience and overall project strategy are developed in concert and are continuously synchronized. With a passion for good design, solving hard problems and working with a wide set of customers, we set out to see if we could do some good.

Ben Parkison

Managing Partner

Ben has worked in the aviation industry for ten years, and during that time has been involved in a number of analytical and marketing tool development projects. Ben has been responsible for development solutions to meet airplane certification, new analytical tools to forecast airplane maintenance and engineering workload requirements, and advanced market modeling and simulation tools in support of customer facing airplane marketing engagements. Ben has studied at Purdue University (B.S. Engineering), The University of Texas at Austin (M.S. Engineering) and The University of Washington (MBA).
