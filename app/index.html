Build Better Applications

We operate at the intersection of business strategy and application development. While not management consultants or your typical development shop, we're a little bit of both, and a lot in between. We help companies build better applications, faster.

Connect the Dots

A team's ability to connect the dots between business strategy and application development rests on their proficiency at building common mental models. Without cognitive alignment among stakeholders it's unclear if everyone's trying to connect the same set of dots. Our solutions help teams speak the same language, share the same ideas, and bridge the communicatino gap between strategy and development.

Our Solutions

Our Team

Ben Parkison

Ben has worked in the aviation industry for ten years, and since then has been involved in a number of engineering and marketing tool development projects. Ben has been responsible for designing solutions to meet airplane structural certification, new analytical tools to forecast airplane maintenance and engineering workload requirements, and advanced market modeling and simulation tools in support of customer facing airplane marketing engagements.

Mike Nash

For eight years Mike has worked in the aviation industry developing new tools and methods for mission critical engineering analysis and customer facing marketing applications.
