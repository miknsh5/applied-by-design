Our Story.

Applied by Design was founded on the idea that analytical web applications succeed when technical design requirements, user experience and overall project strategy are developed in concert and are continuously synchronized. With a passion for good design, solving hard problems and working with a wide range of customers, we set out to see if we could do some good.
